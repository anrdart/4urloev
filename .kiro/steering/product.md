# Product Overview

4UrLoev is an Indonesian e-commerce platform for DIY products and personalized gifts. The platform enables customers to:

- Browse and purchase DIY craft products
- Customize products with personal designs
- Purchase product bundles at discounted prices
- Create wishlists and manage shopping carts
- Track orders with shipping integration

## Target Market
Indonesian consumers looking for personalized gifts and DIY craft supplies, with content primarily in Bahasa Indonesia.

## Key Features
- Product catalog with categories and search
- Product customization tools (Fabric.js canvas editor)
- Shopping cart with Stripe payment integration
- User authentication via Supabase (email/password, Google OAuth)
- Admin dashboard for product/order/user management
- Wishlist functionality
- Theme customization (glassmorphism UI with dark mode support)

## Business Model
- Direct product sales
- Product bundles with discount pricing
- Custom design services
